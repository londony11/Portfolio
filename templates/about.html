My path so far

I got my Bsc. and Msc. degree at the Budapest University of Technology and Economics as a Vehicle Engineer specialized for vehicle manufacturing. I graduated in 2018.

During my Masters, I worked for a year as an intern at EMPA a Swiss Federal Laboratory for Material Scinece and Technology, where I investigated the physical properties of thin film metalic glasses. You can find my Master Thesis on this topic here, and a related paper, where I contributed.

In 2019 I joined AVL Hungary, an automotive supplier company, where I joined to the development various software solutions, which controlled different systems on the vehicle, such as Exhaust After-treatment System, Fuel Cell System, Battery Management System.

Parallel to AVL Hungary, I got a post-graduate Engineer-Economist degree at Corvinus University of Budapest, where I studied Business and Administration topics. My Thesis (hungarian) investigated the effect of financial literacy on the wealth of households.

In 2022 I joined to Jaguar Land Rover Hungary as a System Engineer. I became the owner of the Vehicle State Estimation System, which estimate vehicle motion states and parameters, which is not efficient, or impossible to measure.
